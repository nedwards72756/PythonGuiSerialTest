3.1.6 SMA

[LF]FRNMCSSXXXXXXXXUUS[CR]

Length: 20 bytes
Decimal does not increase length.

[LF]: Start Byte: 0x0A
F: Flags:

  • Z=Center of zero
  • O=Over capacity, Display "O CAP" on scoreboard
  • E=Zero error,
  • U=Under zero
  • space=No flag set.

R: Range: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9. Ignore, Not used for displaying.
N: Gross/Net:

  • "G"=Gross
  • "N"=Net

M: Motion:

  • "M"=In motion
  • "space"=No motion

C: Custom Flags: Ignore, Not used for displaying
SS: space space: 0x20 0x20
XXXXXXXX: 8 digits of weight including decimal and negative sign.
Examples:

  • "-12236.8"
  • "   10476"
  • "  115388"

UU: Units:

  • "  ", None
  • "tn", usa ton
  • "g", gram
  • "lb", pounds
  • "oz", ounce
  • "kg", kilogram
  • "t", metric tonne

S: space: 0x20
[CR]: End Byte: 0x0D

Examples

Postive weight, no decimal, stable weight, gross:

[LF] 1G 1      2210lb [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, in motion:

[LF] 1GM2     10476lb [CR]

Negative weight, no decimal, in motion:

[LF]U1GM1      -523lb [CR]

Negative weight, no decimal, stable weight, gross:

[LF]U1G 1     -4624lb [CR]

Center of zero:

[LF]Z1G 1         0lb [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, net:

[LF] 1N 1         0lb [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, net, with interval of 10 or 20:

[LF] 1N 1        00lb [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, over capacity:

[LF]O1G 1    134946lb [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, using 6 digits:

[LF] 1G 1    115388lb [CR]

Postive weight, 1 decimal place:

[LF] 1G 1   11594.2lb [CR]

Negative weight, 1 decimal place:

[LF]U1G 2     -60.6lb [CR]

Other:

[LF]U1G 1  -12236.8lb [CR]

3.1.7 SB400

Without decimal:

PXXXXXXSUUSMSCCS[CR]

With decimal:

PXXXXXXXSUUSMSCCS[CR]

Length: 17 to 18 bytes
Decimal adds 1 to the length.
No explicit start byte.

P: Sign:

  • space= 0x20 = Postive
  • -=Negative

XXXXXX: 6 Digits of weight, without decimal, excluding sign.
OR
XXXXXXX: 7 Digits of weight, with decimal, excluding sign.
Examples:

  • 6 Digit
    • "  3181"
    • "813701"
  • 7 Digit
    • "1014.73"
    • "  72.11"

S: Space: 0x20
UU: Units:

  • "  ", None
  • "TN", USA ton
  • "G", gram
  • "LB", pounds
  • "OZ", ounce
  • "KG", kilogram
  • "t", metric tonne

S: space: 0x20
M: Mode:

  • "G"=Gross
  • "N"=Net

S: space: 0x20
CC: Weight Status

  • "OC", over capacity, Display "O CAP" on scoreboard
  • "CZ", center of zero
  • "MO", motion
  • "BZ", below zero

S: space: 0x20
[CR]: End Byte: 0x0D

Examples

Postive weight, no decimal, stable weight:

   3181 LB G    [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, in motion:

   3171 LB G MO [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, right after zeroing:

      0 LB G MO [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, center of zero after weight is stable at zero:

      0 LB G CZ [CR]

Postive weight, no decimal, interval of 10 or 20:

     00 LB G    [CR]

Negative weight, no decimal:

-  1532 LB G BZ [CR]

Over capacity, Status changed to OC and weight stable:

 813701 LB G OC [CR]

Postive weight, with 2 decimal places:

    0.15 LB G    [CR]

Postive weight, with 2 decimal places, in motion

  130.28 LB G MO [CR]

Negative weight, with 2 decimal places,

-  72.11 LB G BZ [CR]

Postive weight, with 2 decimal places, with 6 digits and decimal in use

 1014.73 LB G    [CR]

Analog low error, no output is sent from indicator to scoreboard, Scoreboard should NOT display weight.

Scoreboard should timeout because no data is getting sent to it. Previous weight should NOT be shown.

Analog high error, no output is sent from indicator to scoreboard, Scoreboard should NOT display weight.

Scoreboard should timeout because no data is getting sent to it. Previous weight should NOT be shown.

3.1.8 SB200

[CR]PXXXXXXXCSUUSMSS[ETX]

Length: 18 bytes
Decimal does not increase length.

[CR]: Start Byte: 0x0D
P: Sign:

  • space= 0x20 = Postive
  • -=Negative

XXXXXXX: 7 Digits of weight including decimal. Always has a decimal. Zero filled to the left.
C: Status:

  • space=None, No status, Display as normal.
  • m=motion
  • o=over capacity, Display "O CAP" on scoreboard
  • e=Weight not displayed.

S: Space: 0x20
UU: Units:

  • "  ", None
  • "tn", usa ton
  • "g", gram
  • "lb", pounds
  • "oz", ounce
  • "kg", kilogram
  • "t", metric tonne

S: Space: 0x20
M: Mode:

  • "g"=Gross
  • "n"=Net

SS: Space Space: 0x20 0x20
[ETX]: End of Text: 0x03

Examples

Postive weight, no decimal, no motion, gross:

[CR] 075240.  lb g  [ETX]

Postive weight, no decimal, with motion, gross:

[CR] 181250.m lb g  [ETX]

Negative weight, no decimal, with motion, gross:

[CR]-527420.e lb g  [ETX]

Negative weight, no decimal, no motion, gross:

[CR]-565000.e lb g  [ETX]

Postive weight, no decimal, net:

[CR] 001040.m lb n  [ETX]

Negative weight, no decimal, net:

[CR]-012680.  lb n  [ETX]

Postive weight, two decimal places, no motion, gross:

[CR] 0988.38  lb g  [ETX]

Negative weight, two decimal places, no motion, gross:

[CR]-0052.72e lb g  [ETX]

No decimal, over capacity:

[CR]1225596.o lb n  [ETX]

3.1.9 IQ355

[STX]PXXXXXXXUMC[CR][LF]

Length: 14 bytes
Decimal does not increase length.

[CR]: Start Byte: 0x0D
P: Sign:

  • space = 0x20 = Postive
  • -=Negative

XXXXXXX: 7 digits of weight. 6 digits if decimal is present.
U: Units:

  • " ", None
  • "T", usa ton
  • "G", gram
  • "L", pounds
  • "O", ounce
  • "K", kilogram
  • "t", metric tonne

M: Mode:

  • "G"=Gross
  • "N"=Net

C: Status:

  • " ", space, valid weight
  • "I", Invalid Weight, Do not display
  • "M", Motion,
  • "O", Over Capacity, Display "O CAP" on scoreboard

[CR]: Carriage return, 0x0D
[LF]: Line feed, 0x0A

Examples

Postive weight, no decimal, no motion, gross:

[STX]    4253tG [CR][LF]

Postive weight, no decimal, with motion, gross:

[STX]    5139tGM[CR][LF]

Negative weight, no decimal, with motion, gross:

[STX]-   1313tGM[CR][LF]

Negative weight, no decimal, no motion, gross:

[STX]-   1304tG [CR][LF]

Postive weight, no decimal, no motion, net:

[STX]     462tN [CR][LF]

Center of zero, interval of 1, gross:

[STX]       0tG [CR][LF]

Center of zero, interval of 10 or 20, gross:

[STX]      00tG [CR][LF]

Postive weight, 1 decimal place, no motion:

[STX]     0.8LG [CR][LF]

Negative weight, 1 decimal place, no motion, gross:

[STX]-  443.4GG [CR][LF]

Over capacity:

[STX]^^^^^^^^TGO[CR][LF]

Analog low/Analog high:

Indicator stops sending weight data. Scoreboard should timeout.
